Privacy Policy

Your data. Your privacy. Our commitment.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Aurix Club (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit our website or participate in our events and activities.

Information We Collect

  • Voluntary Data: Details you submit through forms (e.g., name, email, phone) when registering for events.
  • Usage Data: Non-identifying information like pages viewed and device/browser info used to improve our site.
  • Media: Event photos/videos which may incidentally include participants.

How We Use Information

  • To manage registrations, send event updates, and coordinate activities.
  • To improve website performance, content, and user experience.
  • To showcase club initiatives (e.g., galleries, recaps) in line with our purpose.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers solely to operate our site or events, under confidentiality. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data we hold.
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications anytime.

Event Media Notice

By attending Aurix events, you acknowledge that photos/videos may be taken for educational or promotional purposes. If you have concerns, contact us and we’ll try to accommodate.

Third-Party Links

Our website may link to external sites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

Contact Us

For questions or requests regarding your data, contact: aurixclub@gmail.com
